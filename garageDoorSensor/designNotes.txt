sensor: use SW200D ,  golden pin downward, switch close

when door is closed, outdoor unit sends signal for "door closed"

outdoor unit sends once per 30 sec.


When indoor unit receives the signal, green , good state, reset bad state after 40 sec.
if receive again, good for another 30 sec.

When indoor unit  the signal, green light , good state

When indoor unit can not receive signal, red light, plus alarm,

signal is :  HHLHLHLLH
